⚠️ IMPORTANT UPDATE FOR DONORS IN OTTAWA & SURROUNDING AREAS

Our National Headquarters at Westgate (1309 Carling Ave) is currently undergoing redevelopment, and we are also experiencing a significant decline in volunteer availability. As a result, the reliability of local pickups has been affected.

To avoid delays, we strongly encourage donors to use our FREE Canada Post drop-off option.

Through our partnership with Canada Post, you can print a free shipping label and drop off your CPAP at any post office at a time that suits you. ZeroApnea.org covers all shipping costs.

Your cooperation helps ensure that every donation reaches a patient quickly and smoothly.
