I Need a Machine

If you have been diagnosed with sleep apnea, have a valid prescription, and are facing financial hardship (no insurance, low income, student, disability, EI, ODSP, CPP, or temporary unemployment), we may be able to add you to our national waitlist to receive a refurbished CPAP or BiPAP machine, free of charge.

Our mission is simple:

You make the request. We make the connection.

To begin the process, please email:

Patient.Support@ZeroApnea.org

or

info@ZeroApnea.org

Please include the three required documents listed below.

Section 1 - What You Need to Apply

To be added to the waitlist, you must provide:

1. A valid prescription (RX)

Your prescription must indicate the type of machine needed (CPAP, Auto-CPAP, BiPAP, or VPAP).

2. A signed patient waiver

This is our Waiver of Liability – Release of Claims (Patients) form.

Download it HERE

3. Proof of Financial Hardship

You may provide one of the following:

  • a recent bank balance screenshot
  • proof of EI, ODSP, CPP, Disability, or student status
  • a short explanation of your situation (job loss, medical hardship, etc.)

Once we receive these three items, you will be added to the waitlist.

Section 2 - Full Process and Important Details

ZeroApnea.org is a volunteer-run community initiative, not a medical provider.

We do not diagnose sleep apnea, offer clinical advice, or guarantee availability of equipment.

Our role is to collect donated machines, refurbish them safely, and match them with patients who demonstrate both medical need and financial hardship.

How the Patient Waitlist Works

1. Submit the required documents

Send your:

  • Prescription
  • Signed patient waiver
  • Proof of financial hardship

to: Patient.Support@ZeroApnea.org

2. Your name is added to the waitlist

Out waitlist is nationwide and grows daily.

Availability depends entirely on donor contributions

Priority is based on:

  • Medical urgency
  • Severity of symptoms
  • Financial hardship
  • Device availability (CPAP vs BiPAP vs AutoSet)

We are transparent:

We cannot predict wait times, as they depend on donor inventory.

3. Matching you with a machine

Once a suitable refurbished device becomes available, we will contact you.

Each donated machine is:

  • inspected
  • sanitized
  • reset
  • tested by volunteers
  • matched with appropriate accessories when available

Accessories such as tubing, filters, and masks are provided only when inventory permits.

How You Will Receive Your Machine (Shipping Process)

We use our partnership with Canada Post for national delivery.

When a machine becomes available:

  • We prepare your equipment
  • We pack it securely
  • We ship it to the address you provided
  • We cover all shipping costs
  • You receive a Canada Post tracking number

There is no charge to you at any point.

Your Responsibilities as a Recipient

By joining the waitlist and accepting a machine, you agree that:

  • You will use the device only under medical supervision
  • You understand that the machine is pre-owned and refurbished
  • You will inform us if you no longer need the machine
  • You will donate the machine back to ZeroApnea.org so it can help another patient
  • You are solely responsible for your medical therapy and follow-up with your healthcare provider

ZeroApnea.org does not provide clinical support, titration, pressure settings, or medical follow-up.

Important Disclaimers

  • We are a volunteer-run initiative, not a company or a medical clinic.
  • All equipment is provided “as refurbished” after cleaning and testing.
  • We do not guarantee timelines or availability.
  • A valid prescription is mandatory for all recipients.
  • The waiver protects both the donor and the organization by confirming you understand the nature of refurbished equipment.

Thank You for Your Patience and Trust

Demand is high and donations do not keep pace with patient requests.

Your patience allows us to keep helping people across Canada, one life at a time.

You are not alone.

We are here to help — as volunteers, neighbours, and fellow community members.

If you have questions or need assistance with your application:
