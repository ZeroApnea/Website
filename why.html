WHY DONATE CPAP MACHINES?

Every year, thousands of Canadians living with sleep apnea go untreated because they cannot afford the equipment needed for therapy. Some have no insurance coverage, while others face high deductibles or limited reimbursement. Many of these individuals live without the restful sleep and health benefits that CPAP therapy can provide.

If you have a CPAP or BiPAP machine that you are no longer using, your generosity can make a real difference. Donating your gently used equipment helps someone regain healthy sleep, prevents valuable devices from ending up in landfills, and supports a compassionate, community-driven cycle of care.

Before You Donate

Please remember that CPAP machines are medical devices. They should only be used under the direction of a licensed healthcare professional and with a valid prescription. Every recipient who receives a device through ZeroApnea.org must confirm medical need and provide proof of a prescription.

If you know someone in your community who may need help, you can encourage them to speak with their healthcare provider or reach out to us directly.

Our Role

ZeroApnea.org is a community-led volunteer initiative dedicated to collecting, cleaning, and redistributing gently used CPAP and BiPAP machines to people in need, free of charge.

We are not a registered charity and do not accept money donations or issue tax receipts. Our efforts rely entirely on volunteers and in-kind equipment contributions from caring individuals like you.

All donations are handled responsibly and with respect. Every donor signs a simple transfer form confirming ownership transfer and release of liability. Every machine is inspected, sanitized, and prepared for safe redistribution.

How to Donate

If you would like to donate a CPAP or BiPAP machine, please contact us at info@zeroapnea.org with the following details:

  • Type of machine (CPAP, Auto-CPAP, or BiPAP)
  • Make and model
  • Approximate age and condition
  • Your name and contact number

Your Impact

By donating your unused CPAP equipment, you help:

  • A person in need access life-changing therapy
  • Reduce electronic waste and promote sustainability
  • Strengthen a compassionate community built on care, trust, and dignity

Together, we can make quality sleep health accessible to everyone.

You make the donation; We make the connection.
