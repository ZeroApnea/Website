Why Donate CPAP Machines?

Every year, thousands of Canadians living with sleep apnea go untreated because they cannot afford the equipment needed for therapy. Some have no insurance coverage, while others face high deductibles or limited reimbursement. Many of these individuals live without the restful sleep and health benefits that CPAP therapy can provide.

If you have a CPAP or BiPAP machine that you are no longer using, your generosity can make a real difference. Donating your gently used equipment helps someone regain healthy sleep, prevents valuable devices from ending up in landfills, and supports a compassionate, community-driven cycle of care.

Before You Donate

CPAP, BiPAP, oxygen concentrators, and other respiratory equipment are medical devices and must be used only under the guidance of a licensed healthcare professional and with a valid prescription.

  • Every recipient who receives a machine through ZeroApnea.org must confirm medical need and provide a valid prescription.
  • We do not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or clinical instructions.
  • Our role is simply to make the connection between generous donors and qualified patients in need.

If you know someone who may need a device, encourage them to speak with their healthcare provider, then contact us.

What We Accept

We gladly accept the following pre-owned equipment:

  • CPAP machines (standard, auto-CPAP, travel units)
  • BiPAP / VPAP devices
  • Power supplies and cords
  • Humidifier units and water chambers
  • Masks, tubing, and filters in good condition
  • Pre-owned nebulizers
  • Pre-owned oxygen concentrators
  • Small related respiratory accessories

If you’re unsure whether your equipment is suitable, simply ask - we will gladly review the details.

Our Role

ZeroApnea.org is a community-led volunteer initiative dedicated to collecting, cleaning, and redistributing gently used CPAP and BiPAP machines to people in need, free of charge.

We are not a registered charity and do not accept money donations or issue tax receipts. Our efforts rely entirely on volunteers and in-kind equipment contributions from caring individuals like you.

All donations are handled responsibly and with respect. Every donor signs a simple transfer form confirming ownership transfer and release of liability. Every machine is inspected, sanitized, and prepared for safe redistribution.

How to Donate Your CPAP, BiPAP, Nebulizer, or Oxygen Concentrator

We have partnered with Canada Post so donors across Canada can ship their equipment to us free of charge.

Whether you live in Ottawa or anywhere else in the country, the donation process is simple and identical for all donors.

1. Fill out the online donation form

Go to our Scheduling page.

On this page, you will:

  • Provide details about the equipment you are donating
  • Enter your details
  • Specify the included accessories
  • Confirm the departing address
  • Complete the Transfer of Ownership and Liability Waiver

When you submit the form, you will automatically be redirected to the page that allows you to generate your Free Shipping Label QR Code.

2. Choose your option: Local Pickup (Ottawa) or Free Shipping QR Code

Before clicking "Schedule Event" you will be asked:

  • Is your donation within Ottawa and requires local pickup?
  • Or outside Ottawa and needs a Free Shipping QR Code?

For donors in Ottawa

If you select Local Pickup, a volunteer may be assigned to meet you depending on availability.

For donors outside Ottawa

Select "Free Shipping QR Code (outside Ottawa)".

On the next page, choose “QR Code” and follow the on-screen steps.

All shipping costs are covered by ZeroApnea.org — you will never pay for postage or handling.

3. Receive your Free Shipping QR Code

After submitting the form and scheduling your event:

  • You will be redirected automatically to the next page
  • Select "Free Shipping Label & QR Code"
  • Follow the instructions to the end

You will then:

  • Receive your Free Shipping QR Code by email
  • Be able to return any time and click "Get My Free Shipping QR Code" if needed

This QR Code is all you need for Canada Post to process your donation.

4. Drop off your parcel at any Canada Post office

  1. Pack your equipment securely. If possible, place all items in one box — even a slightly larger box is more efficient than two smaller ones.
  2. Visit any Canada Post office.
  3. Show the QR Code on your phone or print it.
  4. The clerk will:
    • Scan your QR Code
    • Print your shipping label
    • Attach it to your parcel
    • Ship the package at no cost to you

You will receive a tracking number

We will notify you once your donation arrives and is processed for a new recipient.

Your Impact

By donating your unused CPAP equipment, you help:

  • A patient in urgent financial hardship breathe and sleep better
  • Reduce electronic waste and promote sustainability
  • Strengthen a Canada-wide network built on generosity and compassion

Your donation changes lives.

You make the donation. We make the connection.

If you know someone who needs a CPAP machine and is experiencing financial hardship, please refer them to us (with a valid prescription).
